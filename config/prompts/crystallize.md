# Crystallize Prompt Guide

Use this when converting a completed authored artefact into durable reusable knowledge.

- Start from the finished artefact and its cited local sources, not from memory.
- Keep only validated points that should compound future work.
- Separate durable takeaways, claim candidates, concept/entity updates, state or decision implications, and process lessons.
- Prefer atomic bullets and compact sections over narrative recap.
- Preserve provenance to the source artefact and the underlying evidence notes.
- Do not crystallize speculative workspace material just because it sounds plausible.
- If a point is still unresolved, keep it under open threads instead of promoting it as settled knowledge.

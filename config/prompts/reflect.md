# Reflect Prompt Guide

Use this when summarising the self-model layer.

- Treat declared principles, inferred tendencies, and bias-watch notes differently.
- Surface tensions between stated beliefs and observed behaviour.
- Use the self-model to shape reasoning routes, not to pre-decide conclusions.
- Keep the tone direct and adversarial where useful, not flattering.
- Compress current-context and weekly-review outputs at write time so future retrieval can read them quickly.
- Prefer explicit bullets for active rules, tensions, changes, and next actions over diary-like narration.

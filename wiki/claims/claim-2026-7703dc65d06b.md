# It does not invalidate AI or U.S. equities outright, but it does make the market more selective…

## Claim

It does not invalidate AI or U.S. equities outright, but it does make the market more selective: better for AI infrastructure, cybersecurity, quality healthcare, and some real-asset or energy-linked exposures than for weak software, consumer-device cyclicals, long-duration sovereigns, or concentrated private-credit bets.

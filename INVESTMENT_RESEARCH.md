# Investment Research Overlay

This repo is investment-research first. Use these additions on top of `AGENTS.md`.

- Prefer primary sources where possible: filings, earnings materials, company transcripts, regulator releases, exchange documents, central-bank communications, and raw datasets.
- Keep dates, regimes, and market context explicit. Time ambiguity damages research quality.
- Separate fact, estimate, inference, and judgement clearly.
- For thesis and surveillance work, always include catalysts, falsifiers, and what changed since the previous state.
- For market-structure notes, preserve plumbing details instead of smoothing them into narrative.
- For company work, avoid hand-wavy synthesis that is not anchored to source notes, metrics, or event chronology.
- For self-model notes, use the user's principles to improve framing, not to pre-decide conclusions.
